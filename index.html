Join Our Research Team in Network Security and Machine Learning

Dr. Aksoy invites applications from dedicated and enthusiastic students eager to delve into the realms of Network Security and Machine Learning. We are on the lookout for individuals who bring a strong foundation in programming and a keenness to assimilate theoretical concepts in these cutting-edge fields.

If you are driven by curiosity and ready to contribute to groundbreaking research, we would love to hear from you. To express your interest, please arrange a meeting by visiting Dr. Aksoy's office, or submit your application via contact information provided below. Your application should include your Curriculum Vitae (CV) and a brief statement of your research interests and professional aspirations.

This is more than a call for participation; it's an invitation to be at the forefront of technological innovation in a dynamic and supportive research environment. We eagerly await the unique perspective you will bring to our team.
